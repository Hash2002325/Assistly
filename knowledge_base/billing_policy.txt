ASSISTLY BILLING POLICIES

Payment Methods:
We accept credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, AmEx), PayPal, and wire transfers for Enterprise plans.

Billing Cycles:
- Basic Plan: $9.99/month, billed monthly
- Pro Plan: $29.99/month, billed monthly  
- Enterprise Plan: $99.99/month, billed monthly or annually

Failed Payments:
If a payment fails, we will retry 3 times over 7 days. After that, the account is suspended. No data is deleted during suspension. Customers have 30 days to update payment method before account deletion.

Refund Policy:
- Full refund within 14 days of initial purchase
- Pro-rated refunds for annual plans if cancelled mid-year
- No refunds for monthly plans after 14 days
- Enterprise plans have custom refund terms

Double Charging:
If you see duplicate charges, contact support immediately. We will investigate within 24 hours and issue refunds within 3-5 business days if confirmed.

Upgrading/Downgrading:
- Upgrades take effect immediately, you pay the difference pro-rated
- Downgrades take effect at next billing cycle
- Enterprise upgrades require manual approval

Invoice Access:
All invoices are available in your account dashboard under Billing > Invoices. You can download PDFs for accounting purposes.

Payment Disputes:
Contact support@assistly.com within 60 days of charge. Provide invoice number and reason for dispute.
